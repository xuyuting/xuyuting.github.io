I am a PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. I am working on the statistical analysis of fMRI time series data under the supervision of Martin A. Lindquist. My primary research interest is developing algorithms to estimate time-varying brain connectivity for high-dimensional data.
 
I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Physics from Tsinghua University. My undergraduate research was supervised by Yueheng Lan, and focused on developing numerical stochastic simulation algorithms for large biochemical system.
